About me

Looking backward in my life, computers or more generally IT has always been my main interest.

2015 was the year I deeply realized I was… (let me rephrase that) I am attracted to web or/and software development. I bought books and alongside with w3schools website started to learn my path but I didn’t have a good structure to follow.

Until April 2020 a good friend of mine shared a link to a course on the Edx e-learning platform. That course became or it was my very first web developer course, very well structured, full of tasks to accomplish and It was not easy for me.

I struggled a lot to overcome the simplest task. I saw myself on an unknown battlefield to conquer and I accepted the challenge. A year and half later I was able to build this very site in its entirety.

Every day I wake up, still can’t believe I can call myself … Leonard Cogles Bonne | FullStack Web Developer
