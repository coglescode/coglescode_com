About me

Looking backward in my life, IT has always been my main interest in life, both the hardware and the software side.

2015 was the year I deeply realized I am attracted to Computer Science (CS). I bought books and alongside with w3schools website started to learn my path but I didn’t have a good structure to follow.

Until April 2020 a good friend of mine shared a link to a course on the Edx e-learning platform. That course became or it was my very first web developer course, very well structured, full of tasks to accomplish, and everything else I was in need to really get me started.

I struggled a lot to overcome the simplest task. I saw myself on an unknown battlefield to conquer and I accepted the challenge. The course was not easy and I learned a lot, specially my own personality. A year and half later I was able to build this very site in its entirety.

Every day I wake up, still can’t believe I can call myself …
